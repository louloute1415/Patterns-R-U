Welcome to Patterns R U!
This website offers patterns tailor made for you. Pick a pattern below and give your measurements to generate a pattern that will fit those measurements.
Tips:

  • If you change the measurements, press the "Create Pattern" button again to redraw the pattern
  • Sewing instructions are at the bottom of each page, download button is just above them
  • Patterns do not include seam allowances by default. Press the "Seam Allowance" button to add them
